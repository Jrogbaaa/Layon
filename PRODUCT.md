# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Talent managers at the agency reviewing roster health day-to-day — scanning follower trends across many influencers at once, spotting who needs attention, and reading AI-generated recommendations/briefings to decide where to focus their time. Influencers themselves are a secondary potential audience, viewing their own performance data. The core job on any given screen is fast triage: is this influencer trending up or down, and what should I do about it.

## Product Purpose

An influencer-insights dashboard (You First Gersh) that scrapes Instagram data daily and surfaces roster-wide analytics, per-influencer stat trends, and AI-generated creative recommendations/briefings so the agency can make faster, better-informed talent decisions.

## Brand Personality

Cinematic, nocturnal, editorial — "Medianoche" (see DESIGN.md). The agency watches its
talent overnight, and the interface owns that story: a garnet-black canvas, warm ivory
serif display type, one gilded signal color, and a trading-floor tape. It reads like a
couture house's internal terminal — a showcase of craft that still behaves like a
precise, trustworthy triage tool. Data remains the hero; drama lives in the frame, not
in the numbers. (This deliberately replaced the earlier calm/Kolsquare-like light
direction in July 2026 as a public capability showcase.)

## Anti-references

Generic AI-dashboard tropes: gradient text, tiny uppercase eyebrows on every section, identical icon+heading+text card grids, glassmorphism as decoration, hero-metric-template stat blocks. Nothing that reads as templated or interchangeable with any other SaaS dashboard.

## Design Principles

- Triage first: hierarchy should make "who needs attention" scannable in seconds, not require reading every row.
- Data as the hero: charts, stats, and trend deltas carry the visual weight — not decorative chrome.
- Calm confidence over loud SaaS energy: restrained color use, precise typography, no visual noise competing with the numbers.
- One coherent system: consistent spacing, card treatment, and accent usage across roster, detail, trends, and login — not page-by-page one-offs.
- Trustworthy at a glance: this tool informs real talent decisions, so clarity and accuracy read through the design.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

No stated WCAG level or specific user needs beyond standard practice: verify body text contrast ≥4.5:1, support reduced motion, and keep the interface usable via keyboard.
